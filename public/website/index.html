About Our School

Join Our GHA Family — Where Excellence is Our Tradition!

Great Highway Academy is a private primary school in Libala, Lusaka, offering quality education from Baby Class through Grade 7. We follow the Zambian curriculum and prepare our learners thoroughly for the national Grade 7 examinations — but we believe school is about more than exams.

Reading is at the heart of everything we do. Our motto says it best: "The future starts today — a reader today, a leader tomorrow." Every child is known, valued and challenged to grow academically, socially and morally.

  • Zambian curriculum, Baby Class to Grade 7
  • Small classes with individual attention
  • Daily reading programme in every grade
  • Sport, music and character development

A Message from the Principal

"At Great Highway Academy, we treat every child as our own. Our doors are open to families who want a safe, caring school where children learn to read confidently, think for themselves and dream big. Come and visit us — we would love to welcome your family into ours."

Our Classes

From First Steps to Grade 7

Every stage of your child's primary journey, under one roof.

Baby Class

A gentle first step into school life — play-based learning, early sounds, numbers and social skills.

Middle Class

Building on the basics with phonics, counting, creative play and growing independence.

Reception

School readiness — early reading and writing, numeracy and confidence for Grade 1.

Grades 1 – 4

Lower primary: literacy, numeracy, science and social studies with close teacher support.

Grades 5 – 7

Upper primary: thorough preparation for the Grade 7 national examinations and beyond.
